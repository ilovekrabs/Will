Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/26/2024

1. Introduction

"Will" (Written in Luau Lol) is a Discord bot that provides moderation tools for managing Discord servers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data associated with the bot.

2. Data Collection

Discord User IDs: "Will" collects and stores Discord User IDs for the purpose of logging moderation actions such as bans, kicks, timeouts, and warnings. This data is essential for the bot to function properly within your Discord server.

Server Data: "Will" may also collect and store server-specific data such as role assignments and moderation settings to ensure the proper functionality of its features.

3. Data Usage

Moderation Logs: The collected Discord User IDs and server data are used solely to perform and log moderation actions as requested by the server administrators.

No Personal Data: "Will" does not collect or store any personal information beyond Discord User IDs and relevant server data necessary for moderation.

4. Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any collected data with third parties. All data is kept within the scope of the bot's functionality and is only accessible to authorized server administrators.

5. Data Retention

Duration: Data related to moderation actions (such as warnings, bans, and kicks) is retained for as long as necessary to maintain server order and for logging purposes. Once the data is no longer needed, it may be deleted.

User Requests: You may request the removal of specific data related to your Discord User ID by contacting the bot’s administrator.

6. Data Security

Protection: We take reasonable measures to protect the collected data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. User Rights

Access and Deletion: Users have the right to request access to their data or request its deletion by contacting the bot’s administrator. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable laws.

Consent: By using "Will," you consent to the collection and use of data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Modifications: This Privacy Policy may be updated or modified at any time. Any changes will be communicated via an announcement in the bot's official support server or an update note.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the bot’s administrator at the will support discord server.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws of Bulgaria, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.
